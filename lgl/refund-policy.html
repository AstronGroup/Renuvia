Cartpanda Inc. is the Merchant of Record for all purchases made through this store and manages all returns and refunds. Cartpanda's official Refund & Return Policy is binding and prevails over this page in the event of any conflict.

1. Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 60-day money-back guarantee on physical products and a 7-day money-back guarantee on digital products, calculated from the date of purchase or delivery. Some products may offer an extended guarantee period, which will be clearly shown on their sales page. If you are not satisfied within the applicable guarantee window, Cartpanda will provide a return address to facilitate a full refund or exchange.

2. How to Request a Return or Refund

To request a return or refund, contact Cartpanda at support@cartpanda.com with your order number and the reason for your request. Cartpanda will provide the return address for physical products. Requests are typically evaluated within approximately 2 business days.

3. Refund Processing

For physical products, refunds are issued after Cartpanda receives and inspects the returned item. For digital products, refunds are typically processed within 7 days. Refunds are issued only to the original payment method used for the purchase. Depending on your card issuer, refunds may take 1–2 billing cycles to appear on your statement.

4. Damaged, Defective or Wrong Items

If you receive a damaged, defective or incorrect item, please contact support@cartpanda.com as soon as possible, including your order number and photos of the item. Cartpanda will arrange a replacement or a full refund at no additional cost to you.

5. Exchanges

If you wish to exchange a product within the applicable guarantee window, contact support and Cartpanda will provide the return address and instructions. Once the returned item is received and inspected, your exchange will be processed.

6. Subscription Cancellations

If your order includes a subscription, you may cancel at any time through the order lookup tool or by contacting support. To avoid being charged for the next billing period, your cancellation must be submitted at least 3 business days before the next billing date. Cancelling a subscription does not automatically refund prior charges; however, the money-back guarantee applies to each individual subscription charge.

7. Non-Refundable / Excluded Items

Certain items may be excluded from refunds or returns, including products purchased outside the applicable guarantee window and any items designated as final sale or non-refundable on their sales page. Any exclusions will be identified at the time of purchase and are subject to Cartpanda's official Refund & Return Policy.

8. Late or Missing Refunds

If you have not received an approved refund after 1–2 billing cycles, please first check with your bank or card issuer, as processing times vary. If the refund still does not appear, contact support@cartpanda.com and we will assist you.

9. Contact Us

For any questions regarding returns or refunds, contact Cartpanda at support@cartpanda.com
